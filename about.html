For My Beloved Readers

Hi, my name is Tiara Rizqi Shabirina. I am currently studying at a Polytechnic in my city. Soon I will face the internship and also the end of everything, namely the Final Project. It's hard to live, but I hope this can be a good experience and path for me. I have hobbies that are writing, making videos, and photographing landscapes. As is well known, if I always want to capture everything so I can see my experience at any time. At the same time it becomes a beautiful memory for me, such as when I go out of town, to my grandmother's house, and so on.Besides, I enjoy making videos. That's if I have time to edit it. These days no one can record for lack of ideas. However, to take a photo is much easier than having to record a video. Then I have time to write. If I have something to say, then writing is the right answer. Writing can make me always remember the memories. Someone once said, if writing is a work that will be a relic if one day he has died. Even other people can see your remains in written form. It's beautiful, isn't it?

Well, besides that maybe I'm actually not a person who likes to walk around like most people. But on the other hand, of course, I also have to leave the house for personal purposes. Well, though it does make me a little tired from the inner and outer work I have to do. Actually, I agree that going for a walk is the best solution to cool off and rest when compared to staying at home. If you want to know who I am and where I am, you can contact me. It is possible that we can exchange ideas and provide a great experience. Both good and bad. For me personally, the experience is a precious memory. No matter how bad it is, you will always remember it. So much from me, thanks. I hope your day is always beautiful wherever and whenever. See you later!

Thank you to everyone who has visited my page and loved my work. See you later! <3
